Positive Example 1 of Proper Inclusion: "Expanding Product Suite"
Context
	•	Job Description Reference: The role mentioned "Expanding Product Suite" as a key responsibility.
	•	Original bullet point:
Directed a strategic pivot from B2C to B2B2C for a web3 data marketplace, achieving profitability in under 12 months.     This pivot accelerated the revenue forecast by 3 years by focusing on W3C verifiable credentials on Ethereum’s network.
	•	Bullet Point Updated:
Directed a strategic pivot from B2C to B2B2C for a web3 data marketplace, achieving profitability in under 12 months. This pivot accelerated the revenue forecast by 3 years by focusing on W3C verifiable credentials on Ethereum’s network, expanding the product suite.
Reason for Keyword Inclusion
	•	Expanded Product Suite Was Evident: The bullet point described both a strategic pivot and the adoption of W3C verifiable credentials, which directly expanded the existing product offering. The additional focus on W3C credentials led to a broader feature set for the marketplace, thus expanding the product suite.
Logical Support
	•	Accurate Scope Assumption: The bullet point detailed not only a pivot in market focus but also the adoption of new technologies (W3C verifiable credentials), which broadened the product suite. This provides an explicit basis for including "Expanding Product Suite" as a keyword.
	•	Aligned Concept: Expanding the product suite involved adding new technical capabilities to the marketplace, which fits the job description's emphasis on expanding product offerings.
Why This Inclusion Is Correct
	•	Accuracy and Clarity: The bullet point described both the market pivot and the broadening of the product capabilities through the integration of new standards and technologies, justifying the keyword inclusion.
	•	Clear Representation: This accurately conveys that the candidate was not only involved in market strategy but also in expanding the product's functionality, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of their experience.
