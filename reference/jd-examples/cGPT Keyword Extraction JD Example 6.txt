The Opportunity 
We are the market leader in software products and services for the creative & marketing ecosystem with Creative Cloud, Document Cloud, Express, Firefly and Experience Cloud.   
 
Firefly is a new product, service, and family of creative generative AI models coming to Adobe products that offers a new way to ideate, create, and scale content. It’s a natural, exponential extension of the technology that Adobe has created over the past 40 years. 
 
Adobe has made industry-leading progress in addressing the enterprise need for new, scalable ways to create ideas and assets with genAI-infused workflows and products. Adobe Digital Media’s recent launch of genAI-powered products for enterprises including: Firefly Services (APIs), Firefly Custom Models, Object Composites, Style Kits and unique AI features within flagship products like Adobe Photoshop, Express and Illustrator. 
 
We are hiring a Principal Product Manager to “see around the corner” to identify delightful Enterprise generative and creative products and have a founder mindset to scale those products from 0-to-1. You will own the strategy, development, implementation, and growth of new #CreativeEnterprise and #FireflyGenAI products and services.  
 
The ideal candidate is a seasoned enterprise product management leader with experience building AI-powered, enterprise-ready products. They excel at strategy. They understand how to delight creative teams, and are mission-driven to empower them to be more efficient and effective. They love 0-1-100,000. They intuitively understand how to blend AI science and classic software technologies to deliver real value. They are a systems thinker, and a builder at heart. They foster a fun, fast, inclusive, collaborative, empathetic, candid culture with their cross-functional partners. 
 
What you’ll do 
	•	Prioritize a customer-oriented product strategy and roadmap, based on customer needs, business goals, and technical feasibility. 
	•	Partner directly with customers to deeply understand their needs. Iteratively design and build solutions. Scale the result to drive results for your customers and business.   
	•	Conduct market segment research and landscape analysis to inform your requirements. Stay up to date with new technologies and approaches that can improve your product portfolio. 
	•	Collaborate with cross-functional partners to design, deliver, and scale high-quality products, fast. 
	•	Define product requirements and key performance indicators to evaluate success. 
	•	Spot risks, find opportunities, understand competitive landscapes and emerging trends, articulate implications, translate insight into pragmatic, fast-cycle action. 
	•	Continuously learn and confirm/adjust decision-making using data-driven insights. 
	•	Present to Digital Media and Adobe leadership on new innovations, progress, landscapes. 
What you will need to succeed 
	•	Bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, or equivalent experience. 
	•	10+ years of demonstrated success as a product management leader with experience in AI & enterprise.  
	•	Creative workflows, API/developer business, former engineer, preferred. 
	•	Deep customer empathy, and unbounded curiosity.  
	•	Strong communication and collaboration skills, across functional and organizational boundaries. 
	•	Strong organizational skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and prioritize effectively. 
	•	Excellent “connector” of people, ideas, activity. 
	•	Outstanding analytic, problem-solving and communication skills. 
	•	Ability to context switch across products, projects, and customers. 
	•	Ability to working autonomously, manage through influence and deliver on-time with quality.
