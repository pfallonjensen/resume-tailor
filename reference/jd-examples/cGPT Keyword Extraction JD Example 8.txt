Our mission at Duolingo is to develop the best education in the world and make it universally available. It’s a big mission, and even though we've made a lot of headway, we still have work to do — and that’s where you come in!
Our Product Managers (PMs) have ownership over product optimizations and features, from independently run experiments to major product decisions. Backed by strong analytical skills and business acumen, and inspired by access to senior leadership and transparency into how they make product decisions, our PMs develop insights that lead to new product ideas and Duolingo’s next beloved feature. 
Join our life-changing mission to develop education for our half a billion (and growing!) learners around the world.
 

  About The Role...
You'll be a key product leader in helping shape our AI product efforts. The ideal candidate is a 0-1 visionary who is passionate about introducing AI to the world through Duolingo's new product offerings. You'll collaborate closely with other product teams across Duolingo.

  You Will...
	•	Spearhead the development and implementation of AI-first product features by crafting the vision, strategy, roadmap, and execution to advance AI products at Duolingo.
	•	Drive product features through their entire creative cycle: ideation, specification, development, release, analysis, and iteration.
	•	Lead other product managers.
	•	Perform qualitative and quantitative research to discover feature ideas and validate them before development.
	•	Collaborate with a wide range of collaborators, including teams in research, product design, and engineering.
	•	Be responsible for hitting the key performance indicators you are entrusted with.

  You Have...
	•	A desire to make the world a better place through technology and education.
	•	10+ years of product management or related industry experience.
	•	Experience building generative AI consumer products.
	•	Experience leading other product managers and generating outstanding results.
	•	Ability to use user insights, data, and statistical analyses to inform decisions.
	•	An instinct for creating simple and intuitive mobile user experiences.
	•	A desire to build 0-1 products from the ground up.
	•	Demonstrated strong leadership, organizational, and execution skills, along with proven communication abilities.
	•	Bachelor’s Degree or Advanced Degrees in Computer Science, Design, Engineering, Applied Sciences, or equivalent.
