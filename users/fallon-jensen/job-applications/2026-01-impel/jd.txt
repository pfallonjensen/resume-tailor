This executive position requires leading the vision, strategy, and execution for Impel's Agentic AI Platform. Key responsibilities include:

- Establishing long-term product vision and roadmap for the AI platform
- Building and managing a high-performing product and design team
- Overseeing complete product lifecycle from discovery through optimization

- Cross-functional collaboration with Engineering, Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success
- Engaging customers and industry stakeholders for product validation
- Owning commercial metrics including adoption, revenue growth, and NPS

- Strong B2B SaaS and AI/ML background
- Demonstrated success launching and scaling products
- Excellent communication and strategic decision-making abilities
- Commitment to customer-centric, data-driven innovation
- Automotive technology experience preferred
